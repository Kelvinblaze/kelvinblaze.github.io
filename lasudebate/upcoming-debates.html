UPCOMING DEBATE

LIVDC 3.0 THEME: NATIONAL PATRIOTISM: A CATALYST FOR NIGERIA'S ADVANCEMENT TO A DEVELOPED STATE

Lagos State University Students’ Union (LASUSU) Inter-Varsity Debate Competition has its emergence in 2017 and this is the Third Edition to be hosted by the Students’ Union. With the success of the first and second edition, it is proper for the continuation of the Competition. The objective is to ensure that the Competition becomes a continuous major event of the Students’ Union, and the aim is to improve the quality of education, via debate competition.

The Third Edition of this Competition will feature Ten (10) Universities (including Lagos State University) in Nigeria, to compete in the art of debating. The 2019 LASUSU Inter-Varsity Debate Competition will take place from (date of Competition).

The Competition will include the following:

Preliminary Rounds: Teams will be paired and will debate for two rounds (Support or Oppose).
Semi-Finals: The Best Eight will slug it out at the Semi-Final.
Grand Finale: The Best Four will compete for the Grand Prize.

Each participating Institution will require a team of four (4) members and their feeding (three square meals) and accommodation will be fully funded.
As it was provided in the First Edition and Second Edition, the Third Edition will accommodate distinguished personalities and decision makers in the society to deliver series of lectures coupled with panelists’ sessions.

Competition Modalities:

Essay Writing
An essay not more than 1500 words should be written on the theme of the debate. The essay should contain a cover page indicating the name of Institution, word count, and any other information considered incidental to such writing.

Font style: Times New Romans.
Font Size: 12 point.
Line Spacing: 1.5.
Referencing: footnote - 10pt font with 1.0 line spacing.

The deadline for submission of essay would be communicated not less than Two (2) weeks to such time.

A Team shall contain four (4) members (with both genders represented).
Upon invitation, all teams would be debating in the Preliminary Round which would be a face-off of teams on different topics which would have been communicated before arrival. This round would see four (4) teams proceed to the next round. The four (4) teams would be determined by their score/points.

The semi-final round would see the four (4) teams speak on two different topics (two teams’ face-off on a topic). The topics for the semi-final shall be communicated before arrival.

At the finals, the two (2) finalists would be speaking on a topic which would be communicated to teams on arrival. Teams would be represented by two (2) persons at any time, be it Preliminary Rounds or Grand finale.

Dressing: Formal dressing would be adopted for all presentations.
Timing: Eleven (11) minutes would be allocated to each team (Seven (7) minutes for First Speaker and four (4) minutes for Second Speaker), no rebuttals except where allowed.

The Presidential style would be adopted at the Grand Finale. All qualified teams are encouraged to be on their best behavior throughout the Competition; non-compliance may lead to disqualification.

Prizes/Awards

  • Winner - #150,000 (with or without gifts)
  • 1st Runner Up - #100,000
  • 2nd Runner Up - #70,000
  • Best Speaker - #20,000
  • Best Team Spirit - #20,000
  • Best Essay - 10,000
  • Certificates
  • Plaques
