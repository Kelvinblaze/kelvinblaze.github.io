African Fashion Designers are Incredibly Creative.

But what good is it if nobody sees their beautiful and elegant designs?

Join Now

We identified this problem and began working on building a platform that helps fashion designers showcase their works of art and get customers from all over the world. And thus, Tiwa was born.

All you, as a fashion designer, needs to do is to download the Tiwa app, create an account, and start posting. It’s that easy.

A Portfolio for you.

You get a personal page on the app where all your posts will be displayed. You can share a short link to your page to your prospects when they ask for your portfolio; we call this Quick Portfolio

You can also follow other accounts, and other accounts can follow you if they like what you’re posting; just imagine an Instagram just for fashion designers.

See the latest fashion trends and also get reviews for your works.

Tiwa has a “Trends” section where you can monitor the latest fashion craze on the platform and keep up with the trends.

Customers can also like your posts and leave comments to show interest or compliment your work.

Sell, sell more with Analytics you can rely on.

Customers can make direct custom orders from the app, using a form that you can customize as you wish.

Tiwa provides analytics that you can use to track sales and engagement on the platform.

Be a part of something big.

Our sole aim is bridge the gap between fashion designers and the people who love and are willing to purchase the very best of African fashion.
